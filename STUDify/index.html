Budgeting System

This system allows you to track your income and expenses, and create budgets to help you save money.

Getting Started

  1. Create a new budget.
  2. Add your income and expenses.
  3. Create a budget.
  4. Track your progress.

Creating a New Budget

To create a new budget, click the "Create New Budget" button. You will be prompted to enter a name for your budget and select a currency.

Adding Income and Expenses

To add income or expenses, click the "Add Income" or "Add Expense" button. You will be prompted to enter the amount, date, and category of the transaction.

Creating a Budget

To create a budget, click the "Create Budget" button. You will be prompted to enter a name for your budget and select a time period. You will then be able to add income and expenses to your budget.

Tracking Your Progress

To track your progress, click the "Progress" tab. You will be able to see a graph of your income and expenses, as well as a list of your upcoming transactions.
